ABOUT ME

Andrew was born on November 19,1992 in Anaheim, CA. After graduating from Cornell University in 2015, Andrew went on to pursue a career in sports, working for local southern California teams such as the Ontario Fury, Los Angeles Chargers, and the Ontario Reign. After a career in sports, the COVID-19 pandemic forced Andrew to look elsewhere for a professional career.

In his free time Andrew enjoys listening to hip-hop and R&B music, playing sports, and playing video games.
